About Campus Life

Pax Et Lumen Building

I Have Started going to this school since July 2018

Pax Et Lumen International Academy’s ideals are summarized thus: “peace and light through wisdom and compassion” – (Lumen, Pax, Prudentia, et Pietas). Pax et Lumen International Academy awards scholarships to Kinder 2 to Grade 9 Students. It is the only school that grants scholarships to its qualified students starting from Kinder 2 on the basis of discipline, academic seriousness, and good behavior.Smaller classes are mostly used for more better monitoring and disipline. Events are held in this school for example field trips and valetines celebration.

Achievements

Science and Math Scholarships

The scholarship is equivalent to Php10,000.00 per year and is applied towards tuition payments.

Science and Math Scholarships are given to students who have a final grade of 92% or higher in science and math but who do not have the required final general average of 92% to avail of the Gerry Paras Scholarship. A general Conduct grade of at least 92% is also required.The number of Science and Math Scholarships is unlimited. The school will award as many of these scholarships as those who qualify.



Young Scientist Scholarship

This grant is awarded to Grades 7, 8, 9, and 10.

There are two qualifications in getting either half the tuition fee for free or the full tuition free.

Qualifications for Full Tuition Scholarship: -Average grade of 92% (‘A ‘) or higher in Science -Final average grade of at least 92% -No grade below 88% in any subject -Conduct grade of at least 92%

Qualifications for Half Tuition Scholarship: -Final average grade of at least 92% -No grade below 88% in any subject -Conduct average of 92%
